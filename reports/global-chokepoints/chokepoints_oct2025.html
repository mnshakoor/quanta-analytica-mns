Strategic Risk Overview

This report provides a strategic analysis of the world’s primary maritime chokepoints—key transit routes where global trade, energy supplies, and strategic security converge. These corridors serve as vital arteries of international commerce and geopolitical leverage points, influencing both global stability and regional dynamics.
