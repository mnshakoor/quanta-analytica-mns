Cabo Delgado CARVER Assessment, Aug 2025

Interactive visuals for humanitarian indicators and risk profiling using the CARVER framework. Built for analysts, journalists, and responders who need defensible context.

Prepared by M. Nuri Shakoor, ARAC International Inc. | Updated 8 Aug 2025

The CARVER methodology - standing for Criticality, Accessibility, Recuperability, Vulnerability, Effect, and Recognizability - is a structured analytic tool originally developed for military target assessment. In conflict analysis, it allows researchers to evaluate threats, vulnerabilities, and potential impacts with measurable criteria. This report applies CARVER to the humanitarian and security situation in Cabo Delgado, Mozambique as of August 2025, combining quantitative indicators with visual analytics to support decision-making and operational planning.
